 2019 WCC General Information

      The classical-style Four Points By Sheraton Suzhou Hotel (Suzhou Fupeng Xilaideng Jiudian) is located by the beautiful Dushu Lake in Suzhou Industrial Park. Four Points By Sheraton Suzhou Hotel welcomes guests to its cozy rooms since 2012. It is only a 15-minute drive from Suzhou Yuanqu Station and 45-minutes drive from Wuxi airport. Guests can reach DuShu Lake within a 25-minute walk. Due to a bus stop nearby, this comfortable hotel is perfectly connected with other parts of Suzhou.

      While staying at Four Points By Sheraton Suzhou Hotel guests can make use of climate control, Wi-Fi, a private safe, a fridge and an iron with ironing board provided in the rooms. Rooms come with the lake view. They feature bathrooms with a shower, a hairdryer and terry bathrobes. Dining options at this Suzhou hotel include three restaurants and bar. The Western restaurant serves international buffets and à la carte dishes with an open kitchen bringing guests an interactive dining experience. The Chinese restaurant offers Cantonese cuisine and local dishes in 13 private dining rooms with lake views. The lobby bar is an ideal place for leisure or business activities with a wine cellar and a cigar bar. Parents will find babysitting, a buffet and a kids' club for their children on-site.

      The hotel's 11 meeting rooms with advanced audiovisual facilities and Wi-Fi provide good venues for meetings and banquets of various sizes. During their spare time, guests can work out in the spacious fitness center, go for a swim in the indoor swimming pool or just relax in the spa.
